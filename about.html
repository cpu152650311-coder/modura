About MODURA

Building the Future, One Module at a Time

Founded in 2009, MODURA has grown from a single factory in the Pacific Northwest to a global modular construction company with production facilities on three continents. We've delivered over 500 projects — apartments, hotels, offices, and institutional buildings — across 18 countries.

Our Story

From Factory Floor to Global Scale

MODURA began with a simple observation: construction was the last major industry still building things by hand, outdoors, subject to weather and delays. We believed there was a better way — the way cars, airplanes, and electronics are made. In a factory. With precision tooling. With quality control at every step.

Seventeen years later, we operate four factories across North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia, with a combined annual capacity of 4,000 modules. Our buildings house families, host travelers, and provide workplaces for tens of thousands of people worldwide.

Mission & Values

What Drives Us

Precision Engineering

We bring manufacturing discipline to construction. Every module is built to ±2mm tolerance, inspected at every workstation, and documented digitally. Quality is not inspected in — it's engineered in.

Sustainability

We build for the planet as much as for people. 70% less waste, 30% lower carbon, and buildings designed for disassembly. Sustainability isn't a feature — it's the foundation of modular thinking.

Client Partnership

We don't just deliver buildings — we deliver certainty. Fixed-price contracts, guaranteed timelines, and transparent communication from day one. Your success is our success.

Continuous Innovation

We reinvest 8% of revenue into R&D — new materials, automation, BIM integration, and sustainable technologies. The modular industry is evolving, and we intend to lead it.
