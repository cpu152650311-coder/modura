Solutions

Built for Every Residential Need

Whether you're developing market-rate apartments, student housing, or affordable workforce housing, our modular system adapts to your project requirements.

Multi-Family Residential

Market-rate and luxury apartment buildings from 3 to 12 stories. Configurable unit layouts: studios, 1-BR, 2-BR, and 3-BR options with full kitchen and bath installed at the factory.

Student Housing

Dormitory and student apartment complexes with shared common areas. Fast deployment for semester deadlines — 200 beds in 7 months from groundbreaking to handover.

Workforce Housing

Affordable, durable housing for energy, mining, and remote work camps. Built to withstand extreme climates with minimal maintenance requirements over 30+ year lifespan.

Move-In Ready

Finished Down to the Last Detail

Every MODURA apartment module arrives on-site with electrical, plumbing, HVAC, insulation, drywall, flooring, kitchen cabinetry, bathroom fixtures, and interior doors fully installed and inspected at the factory. Site work is limited to module connections, exterior cladding, and common area finishes.

  • Factory-installed kitchen with appliances, cabinetry, and countertops
  • Complete bathroom with tile, fixtures, and plumbing tested under pressure
  • Electrical system pre-wired, tested, and ready for connection
  • Energy-efficient windows, doors, and insulation installed to spec
  • Flooring, paint, and trim — all finished before the module leaves the factory
