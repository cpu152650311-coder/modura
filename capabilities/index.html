Capability Areas

Everything Under One Roof

Six core capability areas cover every discipline required to design, engineer, manufacture, and deliver a complete modular building. No gaps. No subcontractor handoffs. No finger-pointing.

Building Systems

Volumetric modular, panelized, hybrid, and bathroom pod systems — each optimized for different building types, site conditions, and project constraints. Select the right system for your specific requirements.

Explore Systems →

Structural Engineering

In-house structural engineers design every steel frame, connection detail, and lateral load path. Cold-formed and hot-rolled steel systems engineered for seismic zones, wind loads up to 180 mph, and 60-year design life.

Explore Engineering →

MEP & Building Services

HVAC, electrical, plumbing, fire suppression, and building automation — fully coordinated in BIM and installed at the factory. Modular MEP risers with plug-and-play inter-module connections reduce site labor by 60%.

Explore Engineering →

Materials & Structure

Cold-formed steel (G550, 1.2–2.5mm gauge) for wall and floor framing; hot-rolled steel (S355) for primary module chassis; concrete composite floor decks; fire-rated gypsum and cement board assemblies — every material selected and tested for modular performance.

Factory Production

Four factories across three continents operate 14-station assembly lines with digital work instructions, laser-guided alignment, and inline quality gates. Annual capacity of 4,000+ modules with consistent output regardless of weather or season.

Quality & Compliance

ISO 9001 quality management, ISO 14001 environmental management, EN 1090 structural steel execution class EXC3, CE marking, ICC-ES evaluation reports, and third-party inspection at every project milestone. Full documentation traceability from steel mill to handover.

Why It Matters

Why Our Capabilities Matter

Having all disciplines under one roof isn't just convenient — it fundamentally changes how buildings get delivered. Here's what that means for your project.

Factory Precision

Climate-controlled production with laser-guided tooling achieves ±2mm dimensional tolerance — a level of accuracy impossible on traditional sites. Digital inspection records at every workstation create an unbroken quality chain from raw material to finished module.

Engineering Depth

60+ engineers on staff covering structural, MEP, fire, acoustic, sustainability, and BIM disciplines. Every module is modeled in 3D before production, with clash detection, thermal analysis, and structural simulation completed during design — not discovered on site.

Integrated Delivery

One contract. One project manager. One team. From feasibility through handover, there is no gap between designer and builder, no dispute over who owns an issue, and no delay while subcontractors coordinate. Design-build integration cuts project duration by 30% versus traditional multi-contract delivery.

Global Standards

Our engineering team holds licenses across 18 countries and designs to IBC, Eurocode, British Standards, and Australian NCC. Whether your project is in Chicago, London, Dubai, or Sydney, we deliver code-compliant designs with local engineering review and permitting support.
