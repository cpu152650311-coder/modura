How It Works

From Design to Handover in 4 Phases

A streamlined process that brings certainty to construction. Design, manufacture, assemble, and deliver — all managed by one team.

01

Design & Engineering

Architectural design, structural engineering, and modular optimization. BIM models every module before production.

02

Factory Production

Modules built in climate-controlled factories with precision tooling. Electrical, plumbing, and finishes installed inline.

03

Site Assembly

Modules transported to site and craned into position. Typical 100-unit building assembled in 2-3 weeks.

04

Handover

Final connections, commissioning, and quality inspection. Keys delivered with full documentation and warranties.

Sustainability

Building for a Greener Future

Modular construction is inherently sustainable. Factory production reduces material waste to under 3%, compared to 15-20% on traditional sites. Our modules exceed local energy codes by 25%, and our buildings are designed for disassembly — modules can be relocated and reused rather than demolished.

  • 70% less construction waste sent to landfill
  • 30% lower embodied carbon per square meter
  • Energy-efficient envelopes that exceed code by 25%
  • Relocatable design — buildings that can be reused
