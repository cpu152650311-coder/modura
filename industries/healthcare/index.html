The Challenge

Healthcare Construction Challenges

Building or expanding healthcare facilities presents unique risks that traditional construction methods struggle to manage.

Infection Control During Construction

On-site construction generates dust, debris, and pathogens that threaten immunocompromised patients. Traditional builds require costly containment and negative-pressure zones that extend project duration.

Clinical Downtime Cost

Every day a hospital ward or theatre is under construction is a day of lost patient care and revenue. An operating theatre generates £3,000–£5,000 per day — traditional builds that take 12 months can cost millions in lost clinical income.

Regulatory Complexity

Healthcare construction must satisfy overlapping frameworks: HTM, HBN, CQC requirements, Building Regulations Part B (fire), Part M (access), and local NHS trust standards. Non-compliance discovered at handover can delay opening by months.

Future Flexibility

Clinical needs evolve rapidly. A diagnostic suite today may need to become an ICU tomorrow. Traditional construction locks hospitals into fixed layouts that cost millions to reconfigure — if reconfiguration is possible at all.

The MODURA Solution

Healthcare-Specific Modular Engineering

Our healthcare modules are not repurposed commercial units — they are purpose-engineered for clinical environments. Every module is built in our ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certified factory, with full traceability of materials and processes.

  • Negative-pressure isolation capability with ante-room airlocks for infection control
  • Medical gas pipeline systems pre-installed, pressure-tested, and certified per HTM 02-01
  • Seamless, cleanable surfaces with integrated coved flooring — zero pathogen harbour points
  • Full-height lead shielding in walls, floors, and doors for diagnostic imaging suites

  • Quick-connect service risers for water, drainage, medical gases, and data — no on-site welding

Applications

Typical Healthcare Applications

From a single operating theatre to an entire hospital wing, our modular system scales to meet any clinical requirement.

Modular Operating Theatre

Ultra-clean ventilation with laminar flow HEPA H13 filtration; seamless antimicrobial surfaces; integrated medical gas pendants and theatre lights. HTM 03-01 compliant, Type B ultra-clean ventilation as standard.

Diagnostic Suite

MRI, CT, and X-ray ready with pre-installed lead shielding, anti-vibration isolation, and cryogen vent paths. Controlled temperature and humidity to diagnostic equipment manufacturer specifications.

Inpatient Ward

Multi-bed and single-room configurations with en-suite bathrooms. Positive/negative pressure isolation capability; nurse call, medical gas, and data at every bedhead. HBN 04-01 compliant room sizes and adjacencies.

Intensive Care Unit

Level 3 ICU capability with dual-redundant power, medical air, oxygen, and suction at each bedspace. Dedicated HVAC with 100% fresh air and full exhaust; continuous monitoring infrastructure integrated.

Outpatient Clinic

Consultation rooms, treatment rooms, and minor procedure suites with integrated hand-washing stations. Patient flow-optimised layouts per HBN 11-01; telehealth-ready data infrastructure.

Pharmacy & Laboratory

Cleanroom-grade environments for aseptic compounding and pathology. Temperature-controlled storage, fume extraction, and GMP-compliant surfaces. Segregated clean and dirty workflows built into the floorplan.
