What Happens Next

Once you submit your inquiry, here’s how we move from brief to build.

We Review Your Brief

Our engineering team assesses your requirements within 24 hours, reviewing building type, scale, location constraints, and any special requirements you’ve outlined.

Feasibility Study

We provide a free high-level feasibility assessment including indicative programme, module count estimate, logistics assessment, and outline specification aligned with your brief.

Budget Proposal

Detailed budget estimate with cost breakdown by work package — design, manufacturing, logistics, installation, and finishing. Delivered within 48 hours of the feasibility assessment.

Design Workshop

Optional: schedule a workshop with our design team to refine the brief, explore façade options, review layout configurations, and align on programme milestones.
